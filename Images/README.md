# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) – Image Collection

This folder contains the visualizations generated during the **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** phase of the MSBA Capstone project with MasterControl.

The purpose of these visuals was to better understand the structure and behavior of the lead dataset before building predictive models.

The images in this folder helped the team explore:

- Distribution of leads across **product lines (Qx vs Mx)**

- Differences in **lead progression rates**

- Patterns across **industries and company characteristics**

- Distribution of **buyer roles, job titles, and seniority levels**

- Relationships between **account attributes and sales progression**

- Early indicators of segments that show stronger conversion potential

These visual analyses were essential for identifying trends, anomalies, and potential predictors within the dataset. The insights gained during this stage guided later steps such as:

- Data cleaning and preprocessing  

- Feature selection and engineering  

- Identification of meaningful variables for predictive modeling  

Overall, the EDA visuals served as an important foundation for understanding the sales lead data and for developing the modeling strategy used in the project.
